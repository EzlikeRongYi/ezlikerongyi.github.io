For the love of data and everything inbetween.

As a data analyst at DBS under the Technology in Finance Immersion Programme (TFIP), I am constantly striving to learn and grow in my field, whilst pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Data Science & Analytics (University of Portsmouth). Over the years of learning and working, I have gained a solid understanding of statistical analysis and data visualization and have developed a keen eye for identifying patterns and trends in large datasets. Apart from my day-to-day role and studies, I am also taking self-paced courses in Azure, mainly Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900), AI Fundamendals (AI-900) and Data Scientist Associate (DP-100) to further my knowledge in the field of data science.
